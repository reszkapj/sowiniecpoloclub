Tradition in Every Move

For more than twenty years, we have cultivated the art of polo in harmony with nature. Every day at Sowiniec is a celebration of elegance, precision, and respect between rider and horse.

Our Argentine polo horses are more than animals – they are partners in a dance that blends tradition with modernity, strength with delicacy.
